Navigating the Souks of Marrakech

Just got back from an amazing week in Marrakech! The souks (markets) are incredible but can be overwhelming. Here are my tips for a better experience:

  • Go early morning (8-9am) to avoid crowds and heat
  • Haggle, but respectfully - start at 40% of asking price
  • Use Google Maps offline and drop pins at interesting spots
  • Jemaa el-Fnaa square gets very busy at night - watch your belongings

Anyone planning a trip there soon?

Hidden Beach Found in Phuket!

I just spent two weeks exploring Thailand and found what might be the most beautiful hidden beach in Phuket. Freedom Beach is accessible only by longtail boat or a challenging hike, but it's worth the effort!

Crystal clear water, white sand, and barely any tourists. The best time to visit is early morning. If anyone's planning a trip to Thailand, I'd be happy to share more details!
